Help Topics

This is the support page for Smith's custom jewelry, our frequently asked questions will be addressed here below.

Who are we?

Founded by David Smith in 1988, we originated from a small at home passion for creating customer jewelery. Eventually growing over the years to what we are today. For over 20 years Smith's Jewelry has the go to destination for those who want exceptional service, and exceptional products.

What if the design I have in mind isn't on your website?

That's not a problem! If you don't see a jewelry design you're interested in you're welcome to contact us and discuss what you are envisioning, and we'd be happy to accomidate you.

How do I choose my own jewelry design?

Great question! We're continually growing and expanding our personal jewelry design tool, from the nav bar at the top of the page you can select the Jewelry Designer option, and begin creating what you envision!
