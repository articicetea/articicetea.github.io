Jagged Leopard's Origin

Jagged Leopard was founded in 2001 by Jake Bateman. Owner and CEO Jake came up with the inspiration for his company during a safari trip in Africa. After many sleepless nights tossing and turning, tortured by a feeling of stagnation in his life Jake had made the decision to travel to Africa in search for inspiration.

During the trip Jake witnessed a leopard crouched behind a large jagged rock resembling a glorious earthen tower, the leopard was quiet as a mouse hiding in wait for its next meal. It was at this moment Jake made the decision to not become the metaphorical prey and to embody the spirit of the leopard on the hunt for a successful business.

Shortly after forming the company Jake sought the help of world renowned web designer Jon Lair, and thus a new partnership was formed.
